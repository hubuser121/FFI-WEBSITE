General Rules

  1. No hacks, scripts, emulators or unfair advantages.
  2. Players must join on time; late joiners may be disqualified.
  3. Any abusive chat or griefing can lead to instant ban.
  4. Organizer’s decision is final regarding disputes.

Room & Match Rules

  • Room ID & Password shared 15 minutes before match start.
  • Re-host only if majority disconnect due to server issue.
  • If a squad member crashes, the match continues.

Prize & Payment

  • Prizes will be sent via UPI to the registered leader.
  • Winning squads must share valid payment details within 24 hours.
